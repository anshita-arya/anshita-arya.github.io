About Me

Engineer turned Product Manager

I'm a Product Manager at Hewlett Packard Enterprise with a background in Software Engineering at Microsoft. My technical roots give me a rare ability to speak both the language of users and the language of engineers — which means fewer translation errors and faster, better products.

At HPE, I lead hybrid cloud products driving $70M+ in revenue, integrating sustainability as a core product priority and building 0->1 AI products to boost team productivity and bring more value to Enterprise customers. Before that, I spent 5 years at Microsoft and the Cloud+AI team, building products used by thousands of engineers and partners globally.

I hold a Master's in Information Management & Systems from UC Berkeley and love the intersection of AI, data, and human behavior.
